How To Publish Your Site

Three solid ways to get this website live — including options you can edit from your phone.

01
Netlify Drop

Drag your index.html file to Netlify Drop. It's live in under 60 seconds. Free forever. No account needed to start.

To edit later: create a free Netlify account, link a GitHub repo, and push changes — they deploy automatically.

Go to Netlify Drop →
02
GitHub Pages

Create a free GitHub account, upload your index.html, and enable GitHub Pages in Settings. Free custom domain support included.

Edit on phone: Use the GitHub mobile app to edit files directly. Changes go live in seconds.

Go to GitHub Pages →
03
Framer (Visual Editor)

If you want to visually click and drag to edit — Framer is the closest to what Lovable offers. It has an AI builder and a full visual editor. Mobile-friendly editing dashboard.

Free tier available. You'd rebuild the site there, but the result is fully editable without code.

Go to Framer →

Our Story

About Us

The Beginning

Born From
One Developer's Dream

"Every great studio started with a single person who refused to stop building."

Next Step Studio is an independent game development studio founded on a simple but powerful idea: that great games don't require a massive team — they require vision, discipline, and the courage to keep going.

What started as a solo developer working in Unity is the seed of something much larger. The studio was built to grow — to take on new talent, new tools, and new challenges as each project pushes boundaries.

We don't have a hundred people. We have something better: absolute clarity of purpose, and the freedom to build exactly what we believe in.

What We Stand For

Our Values

⬡
Craftsmanship

Every mechanic, every frame, every line of code is treated like it matters — because it does. We don't ship things we're not proud of.

◈
Ambition

We aim higher than our current resources suggest we should. That's not recklessness — it's how every great studio has ever grown.

◎
Integrity

We're honest about where we are. A solo studio today, building with the discipline and intent of something much larger tomorrow.

✦
Growth

Every project is a step forward — in skill, scope, and team size. We don't stand still. We take the next step, always.

⬙
Player First

Games exist for the people who play them. Every design decision, every mechanic — it's for the player's experience.

◇
Independence

We answer to our creative vision, not a committee. That independence is what allows us to take risks and make genuinely different games.

The Team

The Founder

✦
Founder
Solo Developer · Director

Next Step Studio is currently a one-person operation — a solo developer who wears every hat: programmer, designer, artist, producer, and studio head.

Starting with psychological horror in Unity, the studio is building a foundation of craft and vision that will grow into something much larger. Every line of code, every design decision, every creative choice comes from one place — and that focused, uncompromising vision is exactly what makes Next Step Studio's games feel the way they do.

As the team grows, that founding vision remains the north star. And one day — a custom engine, a custom language, and a studio that answers to no one but its own creative ambition.
